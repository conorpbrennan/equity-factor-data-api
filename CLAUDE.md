# Project instructions

## Report & documentation styling

"Tufte & Few" styling always means the **light** treatment used by
v2-report.html: paper ground (#fffffe), dark ink (#1c2126), one blue accent
(#3b6ea5), 15px system-ui, hairline tables, border-left callouts. Single
light theme only — never produce a dark background or a dark variant unless
I explicitly ask for one.
